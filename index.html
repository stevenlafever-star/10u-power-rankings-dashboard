Methodology

Player data: GameChanger season exports, qualified = ≥25 AB & ≥8 games (hitters), ≥10 IP (pitchers) — filters out one-day guest players. ERA on a 7-inning basis. OPS = OBP + SLG. XBH = 2B+3B+HR. BIP% = (AB−K)/PA, share of plate appearances putting the ball in play. Strike% = strikes/total pitches. K% (pitchers) = strikeouts per batter faced. Pitcher dominance rank = z-score blend of ERA, WHIP, K% (full weight) and Strike% (half weight). Team scores are 0–100 (50 = average of the 37 scouted teams, ±15/SD); SOS-adjusted adds half the team's SOS z-score — SOS = average opponent network rating. Glass City Finesse excluded (corrupted export). Silver Creek is unranked (not in the rankings network).
