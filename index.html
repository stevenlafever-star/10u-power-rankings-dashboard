How to read this

Scores are 0–100 indexes vs. the 11 scouted teams (50 = average of this elite group, ±15 per standard deviation). Hitting Depth blends team OBP with the OBP of lineup slots 7–9 (bottom of order). Balls in Play = share of plate appearances ending with a ball put in play ((AB−K)/PA) — how hard a team is to strike out. Pitching Dominance blends staff ERA (7-inning basis), WHIP, and strikeout rate (K per batter faced). SOS-adjusted versions credit teams whose stats came against tougher schedules: half of each team's SOS z-score (SOS = avg. opponent power rating from the rankings network) is added to the raw score. In the full rankings table, Adj RF/G = RF/G + SOS/2 and Adj RA/G = RA/G − SOS/2. Qualified players: ≥15 AB (hitters), ≥8 IP (pitchers) — filters out one-day guest players.
